Show notes

Vince and Matt talk about random encounters from the New York, New York supplement and how to use those tables to bring street level Marvel chaos to life.

Guest Chris Tyner joins the show to share his thoughts and experience, and there is talk about encounter pacing, surprise scenes, and using tables without losing story focus.
