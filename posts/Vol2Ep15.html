VOLUME 2 • ISSUE 15
Captain Marvel Recap and Death, Dying Rules

March 11, 2019 Approx. 37 minutes

Show Notes

Vince and Keith start this one with a spoiler‑heavy chat about the Captain Marvel movie and how it landed for them. If you want to avoid movie details, this is the section you would normally skip at the table.

After the recap they move into rules talk, going through death and dying in the Marvel Super Heroes game. They compare what the book says to how they handle things in play and give tips for keeping the drama high without wiping a campaign for one bad roll.
