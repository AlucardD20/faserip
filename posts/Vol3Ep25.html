Show notes

DM Matt returns to the show and joins Vince to talk about weaknesses from page 12 of the Ultimate Powers Book and how they shape heroes.

They talk about using weaknesses as hooks, how far to push drawbacks, and how to keep flaws fun instead of punishing.
