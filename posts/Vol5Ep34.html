Show notes

Vince and Golden Buddha celebrate Steve Rogers turning 100 years old, talk through favorite Captain America moments, and look at different write ups for Cap in play.

They also talk about copycat or knock off versions of Captain America in comics, and how those ideas can fuel NPCs and campaigns at your table.
